### 1. Full English Breakfast

*uk-breakfast-001 - serves 1*

**Why it's eaten here:** The most iconic British breakfast, served in cafes, greasy spoons, pubs and hotels nationwide; a weekend tradition and hangover staple rather than an everyday meal for most households.

**Ingredients:**
- 2 rashers (60g) back bacon rashers
- 2 sausages (100g) pork sausages
- 2 large eggs, fried
- 100 g baked beans
- 1 whole (90g) grilled tomato
- 50 g mushrooms, fried
